Welcome to CDC Travels, your gateway to personalized and unforgettable journeys in the enchanting paradise of Sri Lanka. At CDC Travels, we understand that every traveler is unique, seeking experiences that resonate with their desires and preferences. That's why we take pride in curating tailor-made offers, ensuring your visit to Sri Lanka is not just a vacation but a bespoke adventure crafted just for you.

For the digital nomads seeking a seamless blend of work and wanderlust, we offer custom Workation packages. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and natural beauty of Sri Lanka while staying connected and productive.

For those traveling solo or with a group, our FIT (Free Independent Traveler) packages cater to your specific interests and pace. Whether you crave the tranquility of ancient cities, the allure of pristine beaches, or the majesty of mountain landscapes, we tailor your itinerary accordingly.

Babymooners can indulge in romantic getaways tailored to pamper parents-to-be, blending relaxation with memorable experiences. Our SPAsafari packages seamlessly combine the thrill of wildlife adventures with rejuvenating spa treatments for a truly unique escape.

If you're yearning for a break that encompasses wellness, our Wellness Rejuvenation programs provide a holistic approach to mind and body rejuvenation. Explore the serene settings of Sri Lanka while embracing wellness practices that restore balance.

For the adventurous souls, embark on a whale-watching expedition or indulge in bird watching amid the island's diverse ecosystems. Activity-based travel enthusiasts can choose from a range of exhilarating experiences, from trekking in ancient cities to water sports along our pristine coastlines.

Immerse yourself in the art of Sri Lankan cuisine with our Culinary Art packages. Discover the secrets of local flavors through hands-on experiences, cooking classes, and gastronomic delights.

Choose your preferred style of travel - be it slow tourism, staycations, or sleep tourism. Whether you're drawn to the rhythmic waves of the beach, the historical tapestry of cultural sites, or the untouched beauty of wildlife locations, CDC Travels ensures a tailored journey that fulfills your travel dreams.

At CDC Travels, your adventure begins with your choices, and we are here to turn them into an extraordinary reality. Let us craft a Sri Lankan experience that's as unique as you are.
